Displacement

Displacement is the forced movement of people or animals from their homes or habitats due to conflict, disasters, or environmental changes.

Displacement Rising seas, desertification, and extreme weather events force millions out of their homes, straining resources in host regions. Displacement highlights the social and economic impacts of climate change on global populations.

What to do

Planning

Prepare for displacement by identifying land, planning for service provision, and considering extended displacement in contingency plans.

Registration

Ensure that registration procedures are transparent, non-discriminatory, and accessible to all displaced people. Displaced people should be involved in the design of the registration process.

Access to services

Ensure that displaced people have access to basic services, such as food, medicine, and shelter.

Protection

Ensure that displaced people are protected from physical violence and trafficking.
